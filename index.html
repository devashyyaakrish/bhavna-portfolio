The Story Behind the Craft

Bhavna Kothari is a rare creative who thrives at the intersection of aesthetics, taste, and personal transformation. Born with an eye for beauty and a heart for connection, she has built a multi-faceted career that touches on the most personal aspects of people's lives — how they look, what they eat, and the spaces they call home.

Over the years, Bhavna has cultivated deep expertise across artisan tea time baking, personal styling, image consulting, and interior design. Her clients often remark that she doesn't just change how they look — she changes how they carry themselves.

Her philosophy is simple: everything around us is an expression of who we are. A beautifully styled wardrobe, a perfectly baked pastry, a thoughtfully designed room — each is a story told without words. Bhavna helps her clients tell the most authentic version of that story.

A Family Built on Creativity

Bhavna works in close collaboration with her husband, Sharad Kothari, who brings years of expertise in the paints and surfaces industry. Together, they offer a seamless interior design and colour consulting service that turns homes into havens. When Bhavna's eye for design meets Sharad's mastery of materials and finishes, the result is spaces of rare beauty and lasting quality.
